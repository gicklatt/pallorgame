Pallor

Drift, leap, fall in the dark

A one-thumb gothic bouncer about falling — gracefully — through the dark. A tiny skull tumbles down a crumbling pixel-art world; hold to drift, flick up to leap.

One thumb, total flow

Hold to drift

Press anywhere on the screen to drift toward that side. Flick up to leap without losing your glide.

No ads, no timers

Nothing breaks your run. Instant restart — from death to the next descent in a single tap.

Eight little horrors

Unlock eight playable creatures in the Wardrobe — a skull, a moon, an ember, a void, a pumpkin, a ghost, a bat and a toad.

Gothic, not grim

Hand-made pixel art, a shifting moody palette and a soundtrack that changes with every biome.

The descent

The sky darkens the deeper you fall. Each biome is meaner than the last, and each sends its own horror after you.
